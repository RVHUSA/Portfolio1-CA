About me

Hi, I’m Rebecca — a frontend development student with a creative mindset and a love for building things that people enjoy using. I’m currently halfway through my studies, and during this journey, I’ve discovered how much I enjoy creating modern, user-friendly websites using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.

Creativity has always been a big part of who I am, and frontend development lets me combine visual design with logical thinking — the best of both worlds. I’m especially drawn to clean layouts, intuitive interfaces, and accessible solutions that enhance the user experience.
